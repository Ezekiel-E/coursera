This project was carriedout as capstone project for the university of Michigan at Coursera.org.

Introduction

According to Wikipedia Career portfolios are used to plan, organize and document education, work samples and skills. People use career portfolios to apply for jobs, apply to college or training programs. They are more in-depth than a resume, which is used to summarize the above in one or two pages. With tons of definition for portfolio I prefer to focus on a portfolio as an extension of a freelancer’s (or company’s) résumé. It provides a convenient way for potential clients to view your work while also allowing you to expand on your skills and services. (Themetrust 2018).

Aim and Purpose

The Values / Benefits to a Company/Organization/End-user This will reveal potentials to company/organization making understanding/trust between client or workers and company/organization in terms of skills and ability. This will help in productivity when potential are revealed company knows whether to hire a client or not and also get access to the client/worker skills they need. It also give organization clear view to where to fix client or worker. It can reveal a skill set a company never taught they need but actually do.
