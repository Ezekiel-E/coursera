Portfolio Website/App

This project was carried out by and documented by Ezekiel Ediale as capstone project for the University of Michigan at Coursera.org.

Introduction

According to Wikipedia Career portfolios are used to plan, organize and document education, work samples and skills. People use career portfolios to apply for jobs, apply to college or training programs. They are more in-depth than a resume, which is used to summarize the above in one or two pages. With tons of definition for portfolio I prefer to focus on a portfolio as an extension of a freelancer’s (or company’s) résumé. It provides a convenient way for potential clients to view your work while also allowing you to expand on your skills and services. (Themetrust 2018).

Aim and Purpose

Purpose of a portfolio which I aim to develop is to present potentials/expertise and one’s values/benefits to a Company/Organization/End-user. A portfolio website help to increase accessibility of a portfolio and can reach larger number of audience. Other purpose of a portfolio include:

  • This will reveal potentials to company/organization honing understanding/trust between individuals and company/organization in terms of skills and ability.
  • This will help in productivity when potential are revealed company knows whether to hire a client or not and also get access to the client/worker skills they need.
  • It also give organization clear view to where to fix client or worker.
  • It can reveal a skill set a company never taught they need but actually do.

Expected List of Features

  • Navigation: Navigation bar/Navigation medium
  • Contact Feature: This should feature rich accessible element like social buttons and quick messaging tool that help to reach an individual or company.
  • Development Gallery: This should be able to display clickable images or preview of previous project collection. The preview when click should show text and other rich feature for clear information about the project. This should be easily updated, that is one should be able to easily add new work project.
  • Skill Level/Certifiation: This should show/animate one’s complete skill, experience / certification (optional with links). Also may include progress bar feature on some skills, level, and/or services.
  • Profile Section: There should be an area for profile details; a profile picture, profile tag name for quick view of bio details.
  • Network Group: This should show a list of partners, special team, clients and top colleagues if content is available.
  • All feature should be easily updated.

Brief justifications for including these features

Features included are well required for a good portfolio. The Development Gallery clarify your ability by showing an exhibition of the Skill Level which is yet another feature that tell about your skill and experience along with the Profile Section that helps in providing a view of a person who can be contacted easily by the help of the contact features. Hence each feature required to make a complete process of revealing a good portfolio for hire or collaboration.
